Why it works

Built for real workplaces.

Every feature in BetFit is designed around the way teams actually work — and move.

  • Every step and distance metric is sourced directly from Apple HealthKit — the same professional-grade sensor framework used by elite athletes and medical researchers.

  • Background HealthKit delivery means your steps sync hourly without opening the app. Dashboard refreshes happen automatically every time you check in.

  • Row-level security ensures each user can only read their own raw data. Leaderboards show aggregated totals only — no raw health records are ever shared with teammates.

  • Challenges create a natural reason for colleagues to connect outside of meetings. Companies see measurable improvements in team morale within the first month.

  • Every BetFit challenge builds a micro-community around shared movement goals. Friendly competition creates belonging — and belonging keeps people moving.
